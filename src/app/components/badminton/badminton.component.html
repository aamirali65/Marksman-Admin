Badminton Game Details Total Players 4

Badminton is a racket sport played with a shuttlecock (also called a "birdie") and a racket. The objective of the game is to hit the shuttlecock back and forth over a high net, with the goal of making it impossible for the opponent to return the shuttlecock.

Types of Badminton

There are two main types of badminton: singles and doubles. In singles, two players play against each other, while in doubles, two teams of two players play against each other.

How to Play

The game begins with a serve, in which the server hits the shuttlecock over the net and into the opponent's court. The opponent then tries to hit the shuttlecock back over the net, and the rally continues until one player makes a mistake, such as hitting the shuttlecock out of bounds or into the net.

Points are awarded to the player or team who wins the rally. The first player or team to reach 21 points (with a lead of at least 2 points) wins the game. If the score reaches 20-20, the game continues until one player or team has a 2-point lead.

Techniques

In addition to hitting the shuttlecock over the net, players can also use different techniques such as clears, smashes, and drops to outsmart their opponents.
